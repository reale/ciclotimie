# i

Jeremy Mann, %
Times Square Lights%

we hear the mournful knells

the glorious glare of day  
quenched with gloom  
hath yielded to night

the luscious scent of blossoms  
is fraught with rue  
with bitter reproach

and lo! from afar we spy  
the gleamy lights  
and the dismal attire  
of the torch-bearers
